Journal #2 – Migration and Progress

December 3, 2025

Today I migrated PiperLove from Vercel to a VPS. The move feels right—more control, no cold starts, and the engine responds faster now. It's a small step, but it matters.

I'm currently working on the opening table. This is where things get interesting. Opening theory is essentially a database of tried-and-tested positions, and feeding that knowledge into PiperLove should make it play more like a real chess engine. No more reinventing the wheel in the first 10 moves.

The engine already feels like it's around 1000 ELO. That's exciting. It's not brilliant yet, but it's no longer making obviously bad moves. There's a sense of progress—like watching something grow from random noise into actual patterns.

I'm looking forward to seeing how PiperLove evolves. Every improvement, every optimization, brings it closer to what I envision: a chess engine with personality, not just strength.
